About Us

Nonu Restaurant owes its popularity for the food products made of the finest quality of ingredients and served fresh and hygienically. Most of the food products are 100 % vegetarian. Nonu restaurant stand out for its peppy interiors and meticulously planned arrangements specially designed for the convenience & pleasure of the guests. The encouragement and love of our guests have helped us make presence in the prominent locations of the city of Indore. Our Online Store for sale of cake and cookies is a double delight to our guests who are away and for those around too. With Home delivery options too, we wish smooth and comfortable experience of pleasure and joy to our guests. We own stores in Indore only. We have no Franchise. We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.

WHERE WE COME FROM

In 2019, Son borrowed rs6000 from their mom to open a restaurant in Indore. He named it Nonu Restaurant, because their sign only had room for 14 letters. How profound! Soon, the restaurant grew. Why? The dishes was awesome. The service felt like home. And the customers were treated like family. And we’ve been delivering that same food and service ever since.
