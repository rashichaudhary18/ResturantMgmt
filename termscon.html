No-Show Policy

Nonu's Restaurant is committed to providing superior quality services to users and to the restaurants we work with. To assist us in maintaining a consistently high level of service, users must cancel any reservations that they will be unable to honor at least 120 minutes in advance of the reservation. You may cancel your reservation via the automated email/mobile ‘Confirmation’ communication sent at the time of booking or by calling the restaurant directly.

You may also be required to follow the cancellation policy of the restaurant you book with, which is disclosed at the time the reservation is made and some restaurants may require a debit or credit card number to finalize your reservation. In order to use the reservation services for these restaurants, you must provide valid debit or credit card information. To confirm that the debit or credit card information you have provided is accurate, we may place a temporary authorization on your debit or credit card at the time you provide your debit or credit card information. After we verify that your debit or credit card information is accurate, usually within a few days, the authorization will be removed. Restaurant Hub will only use the debit or credit card information you provide in the ways described in our privacy policy.

The restaurant you book with may have the right under its cancellation policy to make a charge to your debit or credit card in compliance with that policy. Restaurant Hub will not have any liability for any charges made to the debit or credit card account due to any failure to cancel your reservation in accordance with a restaurant’s cancellation policy.

If you fail to honor your reservation (and you did not cancel at least 120 minutes in advance) the restaurant via Restaurant Hub will send you an email letting you know that our records indicate you were a “no-show” for your reservation. If you receive a “no-show” notification email in error, please click here for information on how to dispute it. Your Nonu's Restaurant account will be terminated if you are a “no-show” for four reservations within a 12-month period. Users agree that all final “no-show” determinations will be made by the restaurant acting at its discretion.

If you wish to create a booking history, favourites list or rate a restaurant you will need to register an account with us and provide the information we require.

REFUND POLICY

Full refund will be issued in the following circumstances for orders excluding food orders:

  • When the product received by the customer is damaged.
  • When the product received by the customer is different from the original order.
  • When the product does not match the product description.
  • When the order is not delivered or cannot be delivered.
  • When the customer does not like the product after seeing it in person.

Non-refundable Products & Circumstances:

  • Undergarments including but not limited to vests, underpants, briefs, boxers, panties, brasiers, corsets, slips, sports brasiers, all types of lingerie, socks, gloves etc. are non-refundable. It will be clearly mentioned on the product description page. They will be refunded only when they are returned totally unused and in original packaging and condition.
  • Digital media, jewelry, beauty products, and cosmetics are not refundable. It will be clearly mentioned on the product description page. They will be refunded only when they are returned totally unused and in original packaging and condition.
  • If the order is canceled before delivery, it will be fully refundable.

Refund Timings:

  • Customers will have 7 days after the delivery of the product to begin the return and refund procedure.
  • Refund will be issued within 48 hours of the receipt of the product.
  • Refund will be issued using the original payment method.
  • If the order was placed using Cash on Delivery method, the customer seeking refund will have to provide bank details and refund will be issued to the same bank account using online banking or other online payment transfer modes.
  • Once we issue the refund, it can take up to 15 days to reflect the same in the customer’s account.
Our decision on refunds shall be at our sole discretion and shall be final and binding.


ORDER CANCELLATION

You must notify the participating restaurant immediately if you decide to cancel your order, preferably by phone, and quote your order number. If the restaurant accepts your cancellation, no cancellation fee applies. If the restaurant refuses cancellation, (e.g. preparation of order placed by you has been completed and/or delivery personnel has already been dispatched), we will not be able to refund any order, which has been already dispatched. As a general rule, you shall not be entitled to cancel your order once you have received confirmation of the same from the restaurant. Usually, this is within 5 minutes of placing an order.

If the cancellation was made in time and once the restaurant has accepted your cancellation, we will refund or re-credit your debit or credit card with the full amount within 14 days which includes the initial delivery charge (where applicable) which you paid for the delivery of the Goods or the Services, as applicable. We may cancel a contract/order if the product is not available for any reason. We will notify you if this is the case and return any payment that you have made.

However, in the unlikely event of an item on your order being unavailable, we will contact you on the phone number provided to us at the time of placing the order and inform you of such unavailability. In such an event you will be entitled to cancel the entire order and shall be entitled to a refund in accordance with our refund policy.

In the unlikely event that the Participating Restaurant delivers a wrong item, you have the right to reject the delivery of the wrong item and you shall be fully refunded for the missing item/order within 14 days by the restaurant. If the Participating Restaurant can only do a partial delivery (as a few items might not be available), its staff should inform you or propose a replacement for the missing items. We are not responsible for wrong or partial delivery. The issue has to be settled directly with the Participating Restaurant. You can register a complaint with us and if we have similar complaints from other users, we will ensure that such practices are not followed. Any restaurant found continuously following such practices, even after giving regular warnings and improvement opportunities, the restaurant will be removed or blocked from continuing to serve through Nonu's Restaurant.
